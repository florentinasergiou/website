In my Fall 2019 semester, I took a User Experience course. My professor, Regine Gilbert, gave us amazing insights on the centered design process. Topics we covered throughout the course were research, user flows, sketching, heuristics, wireframing, prototyping, card sorting, interface design, responsive design, visual design, interactive prototypes, personas, usability testing, user research, competitive analysis, client needs, and presentation skills. I have implemented these techniques into my position at the NYU MakerSpace to make it more inclusive, accessible, and innovative. Click a project to explore!
